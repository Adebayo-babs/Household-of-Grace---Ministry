About

The Household of Grace, an Apostolic and Revival movement established under the guided instructions of the Holy Spirit for Holy Ghost impartation, Bible Teaching, Healing and restoring hope to the Hopeless. Household of Grace is an interdenominational ministry open to those who are willing to build a relationship with the Lord Jesus Christ.
